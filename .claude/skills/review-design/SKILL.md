Create an agent team for a design review $ARGUMENTS.
Spawn Lead, Methodologist, Skeptic, and Connector.

Lead: present the proposed methodology and experiment plan.
Methodologist: evaluate identification strategy, threats to internal and external validity, statistical power.
Skeptic: is this the right design for this question? Are we testing what we think we're testing?
Connector: are there methodological innovations from adjacent fields we should consider?
Produce a validated design or specific revision recommendations.
